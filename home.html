WHO ARE WE AT THE CREATIVE PORTUM?

WELCOME TO THE CREATIVE PORTUM, This is a platform designed by our convener Kiblat who is presently a 3rd year medical student at the university of ilorin,Kwara State, Nigeria. She is passionate about making creative thrive in their confort skill.
